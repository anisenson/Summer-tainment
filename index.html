About my Summer-tainment

During the summer, luckily, I was able to obtain tickets for the Jets training camp practice. My dad and I had to pre register for tickets because there was a chance it could be sold out, due to the Jets having so much hype leading up the season.

Players I got an autograph from:

  • DB Justin Hardee Sr.
  • QB Tim Boyle

About the Jets

The New York Jets are a professional American football team based in the New York metropolitan area. The Jets compete in the National Football League as a member club of the league's American Football Conference East division.
